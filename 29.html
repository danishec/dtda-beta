Saturday: Toy Story

Saturday morning sunshine streamed into Daphne's room and onto Daphne's toy box! It was an overflowing mess! Dad poked his head in, surveying the toy chaos with a smile. "Morning, Daphne! You could probably get about $1 for each one."

Daphne started sorting through the toy box. There was an old spirograph.. All the wheels were still there. It just needed a fresh set of markers. Twirling with that would be so much fun! To get it, she'd have to say goodbye to some of her toy treasures... Tough choice!
