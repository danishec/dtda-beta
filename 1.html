Meet Daphne

.. a bright 7-year-old with a sparkle in her eyes and a mind full of wonder. Daphne loved making things, solving puzzles, and being a helping hand.

One sunny Sunday, Daphne's parents called her over for a chat. "Daphne," Dad announced with a smile, "We have a fun game to play this week. It is called Daphne the Entrepreneur."

"Onto-po-what?" Daphne giggled.

Mom chuckled. "An entrepreneur is a person who is super-creative in solving problems. They see ways to help people out, make things better or more fun. Remember when you helped Mrs. Gable find her glasses by asking her to write down where she was and what she did that morning? That's a kind of problem-solving, and that's what entrepreneurs do. And sometimes, they can even get money for solving the problem!"
