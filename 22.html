Rocks and Clips!

Daphne remembered seeing beautiful painted rocks in Mrs. Gable's flower garden. "Maybe I can give away something free to make people want to buy the clips!" she exclaimed. She found smooth rocks in her backyard and some leftover paint. She quickly painted 10 colorful rocks. She added to her sign: "Buy a Flower Clip, Get a FREE Painted Rock!"

This was a great idea! People loved freebies! Her busy little stand made people walking by curious enough to also visit. Many stopped to get a free rock, and then bought a hair clip too! Daphne sold 6 more clips that afternoon. She learned that sometimes, adding a little something extra to your product can attract more customers.

Daphne earned a Marketing Wizard sticker!
