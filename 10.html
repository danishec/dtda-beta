Lemons to Lemonade!

Daphne's eyes lit up. "Let's make lemonade!" she exclaimed. Daphne had always wanted to have a lemonade stand. The sign at the grocery store said she could probably make 10 cups of lemonade with the lemons they were selling. She could sell each cup for $1.

Dad helped her buy the lemons. Back home, they squeezed the lemons, added water and sugar, and made a big pitcher of delicious lemonade. Daphne used the glitter crayons she got for her Birthday to make a colorful sign that said "Daphne's Delicious Lemonade - $1!"
