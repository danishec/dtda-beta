Sell and Save

Daphne carefully chose three toys she no longer played with: the board game she'd played many times, the Spirograph and an excavator she hadn't dug out of her chest in a year. She put them in a box and wrote a big '$1 Each' sign on the front with a teal marker. Happy that her toy chest now closed neatly, she picked up the box and placed it in the front yard.

Daphne then went about tossing a frisbee with Daiyan. A few neighbors came by, and Daphne ended up selling all three toys! She now had an extra $3. She felt a buzz of excitement seeing her piggy bank getting fuller!

Daphne earned a Helping Hand sticker for cleaning her toy chest!
