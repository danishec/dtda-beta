Rocks and Roses!

Daphne remembered seeing beautiful painted rocks in Mrs. Gable's flower garden. "Maybe I can give away something free to make people want to buy the clips!" she exclaimed. She found some smooth rocks in her backyard and some leftover paint. She quickly painted 10 colorful rocks. She added to her sign: "FREE Painted Rock with a Clip!"

This was a great idea! People loved the word FREE! And the small crowd of customers drew even more, curious about the fuss. Many stopped to get a free painted rock, and then bought a hair clip! Daphne sold 6 more clips that afternoon at $2 each. She learned that offering something FREE could be a great way to attract people to buy something else. Daphne earned a Marketing Wizard sticker!
