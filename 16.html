Wednesday: $2 Hair Clips

Wednesday morning, Daphne set up a small table in her front yard with a sign: "Daphne's Flower Hair Clips - $2 each!" She proudly displayed her 10 clips, ready to sell them all!

But only two people stopped by. A neighbor bought one for her granddaughter. And after two hours, Daphne had only sold the one clip. She felt a little discouraged.

Dad came outside. "Hey Daphne, how's business?" he asked gently.

Daphne sighed. "Not so good. I only sold one. Maybe $2 is too much? Or maybe people just don't want flower clips?"

Dad nodded thoughtfully. "Hmm, let's think like entrepreneurs. You've got some beautiful clips, but maybe we need to try a few things. What could you do to sell more?"
