Thursday: Cinnamon

Daphne gently stroked Cinnamon's soft fur. "Time for a trip to the pet store, little buddy," she whispered to her guinea pig. Cinnamon's hay bin was almost empty, and his cage litter needed changing. As much as she was loving the Entrepreneur Adventure, Daphne decided to take a break to care for Cinnamon! Daphne asked Dad to drive her to PetsPlus.
